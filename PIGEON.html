Facts About Pigeons

Fact 1

Pigeons have the ability to recognize themselves in the mirror, a trait previously only attributed to humans and a select few other animals.

Fact 2

Pigeons are incredibly skilled at navigation and have been used for centuries to carry messages over long distances. They use the Earth's magnetic field to help them navigate.

Fact 3

Pigeons are able to locate sources of water from miles away and are able to detect changes in the Earth's magnetic field to help them find their way back home.
